About Me

I am Lars Weigmann, a photographer from Halle (Saale), Germany. My work focuses on capturing the essence of places and moments. Through my lens, I aim to tell stories and evoke emotions, blending visuals with ambient sounds that transport you to the scenes I’ve witnessed.
